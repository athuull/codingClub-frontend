Getting Started



Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced coder, we have something for everyone:


  • Are you new to coding? No worries! We offer a beginner-friendly guide to help you get started on your coding journey.
  • If you're already coding, we have resources and challenges to further enhance your skills.

Weekly Updates



Stay tuned for our weekly updates, where we'll bring you:


  • Subject Notes: Downloadable notes and slides for all subjects based on the course syllabus. Login to access the Library section.
  • Tutorials: Step-by-step guides on various programming languages, tools, and technologies.
  • Tips and Tricks: Proven strategies to streamline your coding process and troubleshoot common issues.
  • Fun Activities: Engaging coding challenges, projects, and even coding-related games to keep things exciting.

Community Interaction



Join our coding club and connect with fellow coding enthusiasts:


  • Share your projects, seek help, and collaborate on exciting coding endeavors.
  • Participate in coding competitions and hackathons organized by our club.

About Us

We are the pioneering Coding Club at Rajagiri Viswajyothi College of Arts and Applied Sciences, proudly representing the first BCA batch. Our mission is to foster a passion for coding and technology among students.

At our club, we explore, learn, and code together to unlock the endless possibilities of the digital world. Join us on our journey of innovation and creativity!
