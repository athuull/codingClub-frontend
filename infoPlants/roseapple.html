About Rose Apple



The Rose Apple, scientifically known as Syzygium samarangense, is a bell-shaped fruit that may be red, yellow, or green in color. Despite its name, it resembles a guava more than a rose or apple. Here is some information about the Rose Apple:

Botanical Name

Syzygium samarangense

Family

Myrtaceae

Features

Rose Apple is crisp from outside with a sweet and slightly bitter flavor when ripened. It has a distinct flavor, fragrance, and texture, different from guava or apple. The fruit is rich in minerals vital for heart health.

Health Benefits

Rose apples are known for their high potassium and sodium content, which helps control cholesterol levels. This reduces the risk of severe conditions like heart attacks, hypertension, and stroke. Additionally, they are beneficial for improving immunity.
